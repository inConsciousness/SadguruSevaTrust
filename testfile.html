Ayyappa Temple

The Temple of Lord Ayyappa is a sacred place of worship. It is known for its spiritual ambiance and beautiful architecture. The temple also features the Asta Dasha incarnations of Lord Ayyappa.

27 Nakshatras

The Sanctum of 27 Nakshatras represents the lunar mansions in Hindu astrology. Each Nakshatra is associated with specific characteristics and influences. This sanctum is a place for spiritual connection and astrological significance.

What is Sadguru Seva Charitable Trust?

The Sadguru Seva Charitable Trust is dedicated to providing support and care in various sectors including education, social welfare, and empowerment. Our mission is to make a positive impact through a range of activities.

Orphanage

Our orphanage provides a safe and nurturing environment for children in need. We focus on their education, health, and overall well-being to ensure a brighter future.

Old Age Home

Our old age home offers comfort and care to elderly individuals. We provide various services to enhance their quality of life and ensure they feel valued and respected.

Social Welfare Activities

We are involved in numerous social welfare activities including community outreach, disaster relief, and support for marginalized groups.

Education

Our educational programs aim to provide quality learning opportunities for underprivileged children and adults, fostering growth and development.

Women Empowerment

We promote women's rights and provide support through various initiatives designed to empower women and girls in our community.
